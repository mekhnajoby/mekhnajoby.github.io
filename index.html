RedCycle

RedCycle is a Mars habitat recycling simulator developed for the NASA Space Apps Challenge 2025 – Jezero Crater Waste Reuse theme. It models a constrained habitat’s circular waste management system, focusing on reuse-first strategies, energy and water optimization, and spatial visualization of recyclable materials on Mars.

Key Features:

  • Simulates material recovery through configurable processing modules
  • Implements reuse-first waste pooling and secondary recovery logic
  • Eco and Ultra Eco modes to minimize resource inputs
  • Interactive Mars map visualizing waste distribution and weighted centroids
